Call by Color – made for iPhones

Get direct access to your 12 most important phone numbers. Call your friends and your family members with a single touch. Specify a name and a phone number for each tile in the iPhone preferences.

Features:

  • store up to 12 names and phone numbers
  • independent from your address book
  • use individual nicknames
  • change colors by shaking your iPhone
  • no ads
